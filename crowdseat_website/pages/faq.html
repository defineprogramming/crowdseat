Frequently Asked Questions

What is CrowdSeat?

CrowdSeat is a ticketing platform that focuses on lower fees, fan experience, and venue partnerships.

How does CrowdSeat charge lower fees?

We charge just a small per-ticket processing fee with no hidden fees. This is a big selling point to fans and venues.

How does CrowdSeat enhance the fan experience?

We make the buying experience quick, intuitive, and integrated with social features like connecting with friends also going to an event. We also have an engaging fan community component.

How does CrowdSeat work with venues?

We work closely with venues to promote their events and build their audiences. We provide venue owners with extra promotion and analytics to fill more seats. We also offer a revenue share model.

What is CrowdSeat's business model?

We charge a small per-ticket processing fee to fans, offer a revenue share model to partner venues, sell event sponsorships and fan data analytics to brands, and have the future option to sell VIP packages/upsells for superfans.
