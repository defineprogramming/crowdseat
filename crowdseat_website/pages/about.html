About CrowdSeat

CrowdSeat is a ticketing platform that aims to revolutionize the event experience for fans and venues alike. Our key differentiators include:

  • Lower fees: We charge just a small per-ticket processing fee, with no hidden fees.
  • Fan experience focus: Our platform is designed to make the buying experience quick, intuitive, and integrated with social features.
  • Venue partnerships: We work closely with venues to promote their events and build their audiences.

Our business model is simple and transparent:

  • We charge a small per-ticket processing fee to fans.
  • We offer a revenue share model to partner venues.
  • We sell event sponsorships and fan data analytics to brands.
  • We also have the future option to sell VIP packages/upsells for superfans.

Our technical features include:

  • FAST ticket buying experience with one-click purchase and integrated payment processing.
  • Seating charts and interactive venue maps to easily pick and purchase seats.
  • Integration with social platforms to see which friends are going, post about an event, etc.
  • Robust event management and analytics for venues to optimize their events.
  • Flexible API and white label options for third-party integrations.
  • Strong security, fraud protection, and customer support.
  • Mobile-first responsive design suitable for web and app.
  • Backend CMS for venues and CrowdSeat administrators to manage events, seating, etc.
