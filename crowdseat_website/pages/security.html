Our Commitment to Security

At CrowdSeat, we take your security seriously. We have implemented robust security measures to ensure your data is protected and your ticket buying experience is safe.

Secure Transactions

All transactions on CrowdSeat are encrypted using secure socket layer technology (SSL) and stored with an AES-256 encryption. We follow all PCI-DSS requirements and implement additional generally accepted industry standards.

Fraud Protection

We have advanced fraud detection measures in place to protect you from fraudulent transactions. Our system continuously monitors for suspicious activity and our dedicated security team is always on alert to respond to any potential threats.

Privacy Protection

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. Our Privacy Policy explains how we handle your personal information. We encourage you to read it to understand our practices.
